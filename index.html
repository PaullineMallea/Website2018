Families For Freedom
About

Families for Freedom is a New York-based multi-ethnic human rights organization by and for families facing and fighting deportation. An organization that focuses on immigration rights and puts out yearly reports.

I've worked with their small team to develop brand-centric graphic design executions across a wide range of applications. In 2016, I designed, engineered, and launched an interactive website showcasing their latest legal report written by NYU and Families For Freedom: "THE WRIT OF HABEAS CORPUS: How a United States District Court Circumvents Oversight of Unlawful Detention."

Follow The Honey
About

Follow The Honey Inc. is a small retail company with a big mission. The founders travel the world to bring "transparency of source" honeys and bee-inspired offerings to the market. The mission at its core is to foster business relationships that stimulate economic development through "human rights" honey. Sharing a narrative of a collective humanity through honey & bees.

My Journey with Follow The Honey began as a Project Manager. Working with the founders and a small team to launch the brick and mortar retail store located in Cambridge, MA. Since then, I have assisted in a number of business projects and campaigns.

From developing the brand strategy to creating brand-centric graphic & web design executions across a wide range of applications. In 2014, I collaborated with Follow The Honey’s manager and lead artist to design an on brand web presence that enhanced the customer’s user experience. Designing, developing, and launching heir eCommerce store.
