My experience ranges from digital design to management. Currently, I am the web production and content manager for BeautifulNow, a New York City-based online publishing company. And I continue to freelance as a web designer and developer.

My story

I started my career in the digital realm while working at a startup in Cambridge, MA. At that time, I was the project manager of Follow The Honey. Managing all aspects of the business for their opening day. It was an exhilarating experience and I soon fell in

love with the company and it's purpose [raise awareness for honeybees!]. Without hesitation, I signed on with them to be their store manager. My responsibilities included creating systems that would ensure that the shop would continue to enhance their presence in-store and online. Needless to say, helping them develop their brand and online presence became my specialty. Soon after, I began freelancing and developing my skills as a digital maven. Since then, I have gained a vast range of skills. Because I have worn many hats as a designer, marketer, developer, manager, producer. I see myself as a Jill of all trades and a master of understanding how to get a project off the ground.
